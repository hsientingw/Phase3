Interesting Findings from the FIFA dataset

Physicality of players is the most important quality

Out of the 32 countries qualified for the 2014 Fifa World Cup, a player's physical attribute is the most important quality, it takes into account the player's acceleration, speed, stamina, strength, balance, agility and jumping. Overall, it is the most highly ranked among the players, as seen from the centre point of 64.8 calculated from the median. The spread of the different skills is the largest in ball skills with a range of 18 points. and smallest in goal keeper with around 10 points. Disregarding goalkeeping, defence is actually the weakest quality overall of the countries qualified, with a maximum score of only 49.9.

Higher rated countries tend to have higher average age

As we can see from the bubble chart, representing number of players, over rating and age, the top left corner represents a younger group with higher ratings, these consists of mostly European countries, such as Germany, Belgium, France, Greece. The bottom right corners we see an older group with lower ratings, such as Spain, Ecuador, Mexico. The countries to the right tend to have higher ratings, which is probably because players that are doing well in their career will continue for longer, and players that aren't doing so well (bottom left), will quit earlier, such as Nigeria.,

England has the most players registered with FIFA

Out of the 32 FIFA world cup qualified countries, England has the most number of players with 1618, which is around 600 players more then the next most Argentina and Spain. Iran is the country with the least amount of players with only 11, next up is Honduras with 16.

Number of players does not suggest a higher overall rating

Evident from the scatter plot, Europe has the most players registered with FIFA, with the least being Asia and Oceania, which only consists of Australia. Also from the graph, we can see that more players does always indicate a higher rating. for example, an African country has one of the smallest amount of players but the highest average rating.

Africa is the highest continent in player skills

Overall, Africa and South America has the highest ratings. The lowest out of 6 continents is Oceania with an overall rating of 62.5. In terms of individual skills, again Africa has the highest rating for every skills other than mental, which includes aggression, reactions, vision..etc. The overall worst performed skill, defence, is done the best by Europe and North America and worst by Asia. The overall best performed skill, physical, is best in Africa and North America, worst in Oceania. Similarly with shooting skills.
