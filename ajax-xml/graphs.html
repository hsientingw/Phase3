Physicality of players is the most important quality

Out of the 31 countries qualified for the 2014 Fifa World Cup, a player's physical attribute is the most important quality, it takes into account the player's acceleration, speed, stamina, strength, balance, agility and jumping. Overall, it is the most highly ranked among the players.

Disregarding goalkeeping, defence is actually the weakest quality overall of the countries qualified, with a maximum score of only 49.9
